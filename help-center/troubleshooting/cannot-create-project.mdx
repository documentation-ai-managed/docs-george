---
title: Cannot Create Project
description: Fix issues when creating a project in George.
---

## Symptoms

- "Create Project" button is disabled
- Error: "Project creation failed"

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Make sure you have the "Project Creator" role in your workspace settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    Log out and log in again to reset your session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues persist, reach out via support@georgeapp.com.
  </Step>
</Steps>
