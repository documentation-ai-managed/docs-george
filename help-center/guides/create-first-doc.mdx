---
title: Create Your First Documentation Page
description: How to make your first doc in George.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your project">
    Click your project name in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add new doc">
    Click the "+ New Doc" button in the toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter content">
    Fill in your doc title and main content. Docs autosave as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Press "Publish" when ready. Your documentation is now live!
  </Step>
</Steps>
