## Quick Start

Create your workspace, add your first project, and invite your team.

1. Go to `https://georgeapp.com/signup`
2. Create an account and verify your email
3. Log in and click "Create Project"
4. Name your project and select a template (optional)
5. Invite team members via email

Ready! Now you can begin writing docs and organizing them with George.
